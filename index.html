Bishop

29

The cat's name is Bishop and he's two years old. He also responds to the nicknames Ñango, Zé Pequeno, Gabo, and Faust. His wellbeing is my top priority, and this project was designed around his eating habits and health needs.

I've set a global cooldown between food donations to prevent overfeeding and give him time to digest. Bishop currently needs 55g of food daily, with each donation dispensing 5g. This ensures he can't be overfed from donations alone. The server tracks all donations throughout the day. Before going to sleep, I provide Bishop with the remainder of his daily food intake, typically as wet food or treats, ensuring he consistently receives the proper amount. Donations are disabled during nighttime hours to protect his (and my) sleep.
